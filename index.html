About Me

Hi! I am an Information Systems Technology and Design (ISTD) graduate specializing in Artificial Intelligence and UI/UX design at the Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD). I have a keen interest in mobile and web design but also love working with people and managing projects.

I am highly self-motivated, inquisitive and growth-oriented because I love learning new things. Other than possessing technical knowledge in software engineering and programming languages, I pride myself in being a fast and adaptive learner with strong analytical and verbal communication skills.

I have always believed in the power of imagination and continue to adopt that mentality in pursuing the things I love in life. I am a strong advocate and participant of the Visual and Performing Arts, demonstrating great zest and talent for singing, dancing, music, writing, drawing and photography. Sharing the joy with people who share the same passion just makes it better.

I can be contacted via LinkedIn if you would like to get in touch.
